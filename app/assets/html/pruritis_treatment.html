Pruritus Treatment

First-line

[Nalbuphine (lexicomp)]

  1. Max recommended dosing interval is 0.1 mg/kg every 6 hours, not to exceed 5 mg/dose.

Second-line

[Naloxone (lexicomp)]

  1. Given as infusion at 0.25 mcg/kg/hr.

  2. At recommended dose, analgesia is not affected.

Third-line

[Diphenhydramine (lexicomp)]

  1. Most opioid induced pruritus is not due to histamine release.

  2. Use only for urticaria, wheezing, erythematous pruritic rash associated with opioid-induced histamine release.

  3. May be sedating. Do not give within 2 hrs of an opioid or other CNS depressant.
