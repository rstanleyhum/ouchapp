Opioids

Guidelines

  1. Ensure morphine/oxycodone order is for immediate release

  2. Morphine and hydromorphone are NOT dosed the same

  3. Monitor RR and SpO2 during and after IV bolus.

  4. Use cautiously with CNS depressants like benzos and anti-histamines

  5. Always reduce calculated dose by 25-50% to account for incomplete cross-tolerance.

Equivalency

1 mg PO oxycodone = 1.3 mg IV morphine = 0.2 IV hydromorphone

Contraindications

  1. Hypersensitivity to morphine sulfate or any component

  2. Severe respiratory depression in a setting without resuscitative equipment or appropriate monitoring.

  3. Acute or severe asthma

  4. Use caution in patients with hypercarbia or paralytic ileus.

Routes of Administration

  • IV - Morphine, Hydromorphone

  • PO = Morphine, Oxycodone
