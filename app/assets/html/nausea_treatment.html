Nausea Treatment

Rotate opioid for initial management.

First-line

[Ondansetron (lexicomp)]

Second-line

[Granisetron (lexicomp)]

  1. Ondansetron and granisetron may increase QTc. Granisetron needs pain or oncology approval.

Third-line

[Metoclopramide (lexicomp)]

  1. Most effective if GI motility is impaired. Side effects include extrapyramidal effects.

    [Hydroxyzine (lexicomp)]

  2. May be sedating

    [Lorazepam (lexicomp)]

  3. Effective for anticipatory nausea associated with anxiety. May be sedating. Limited efficacy in chemotherapy-related nausea and vomitting.
