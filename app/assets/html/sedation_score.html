m-POSS (Sedation Score)

Score Sedation Status

0

Awake and alert

1

Responsive to verbal stimuli

2

Responsive to tactile stimuli

3

Non-responsive to tactile stimuli

4

Natural sleep

  1. S and 0 to 1 is acceptable

  2. Stop opioid if 2 to 3

  3. Sedation precedes respiratory depression except in neonate, patients on methadone, and patients with sleep apnea

Journal of PeriAnesthesia Nursing, Vol 24, No 3 (June), 2009:pp 186-190 Pasero C.
