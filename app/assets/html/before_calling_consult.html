Before Calling a Consult

Basic Info

Patient name, MRN, DOB, weight, location, allergies, reason for consult, requesting attending, PCA info (if applicable)

Medical Info

Primary/secondary diagnosis, CC/brief HPI, relevant medical surgical history (if post-op know date of surgery, procedure name, and surgeon’s name).

Pain Info

Pain evaluation, current and home meds, dose, time given, intervals, total daily dose (TDD) of opioids, response, mental status and sedation level

Exam

Time and details of most recent evaluation by medical/urgical team and by you

N.B. Understand that the Pain Team will see patient as soon as practical. In interim, ask for PRN meds if needed.
