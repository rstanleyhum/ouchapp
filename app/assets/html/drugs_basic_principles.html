Drugs: Basic Principles

  1. No one dose fits all. Adjust based on clinical situtation.

  2. "Start low, go slow" beginning at 1/4 to 1/2 recommended dose.

  3. Re-evaluation every 10 min and re-dose as needed until pain relieved

  4. Assess pain before and after analgesia dosing.

  5. Be careful about decimals, calculations and unit conversions.

  6. Do not administer sedating drugs together. Space dosing of such drugs 2 hours apart to see peak effect.

  7. Remember first pass effect when changing the route of administration.

  8. Contrainducations always include hypersensitivity to any drug or component.
