Constipation treatment

Prophylaxis

  • [Docusate (lexicomp)]

  • [Senna (lexicomp)]

Treatment

  1. Rule out obstruction.

  2. Reduce/stop opioid with adjuvant use.

  3. Intensify prophylactic regimen

    • [Polyethylene Glycol (lexicomp)]

    • [Enema (lexicomp)]

    • [Glycerin Suppository (lexicomp)]

  4. Enema and suppository contraindictations include neutropenia, thrombocytopenia, other immune compromise.

  5. Check with primary (surgical or GI) team before treating constipation.
