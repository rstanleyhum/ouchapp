Methadone

  1. Check EKG for baseline QTc interval

  2. May be associated with sudden respiratory depression or ventricular or ventricular arrhythmias

  3. Caution when used with other inhibitors or inducers of cytochrome p450.

  4. Follow electrolytes.

  5. Caution with macrolide antibiotics.

  6. Recommended drug-drug interaction chart consultation.

  7. Used only under pain service recommendation.

  8. When converting from other opiod, initially decrease methadone dose by 90%.
