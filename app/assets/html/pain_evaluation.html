Pain Evaluation: SOCRATES

  1. Site: Where is the site of pain? If multiple sites, where is the site of maximal pain?

  2. Onset: When did the pain start, was it gradual or progressive, regressivee, chronic (> 3x in 3 months) or acute?

  3. Character: Aching? Stabbing? Burning? Tingling? Lancinating

  4. Radiation: Does the pain radiate anywhere?

  5. Associations: Any other signs or symptoms associated with the pain?

  6. Time course: Does the pain follow any pattern?

  7. Exacerbating and alleviating: Does anything change the pain?

  8. Severity: How bad is the pain? See previous page
