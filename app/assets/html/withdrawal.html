Withdrawal

General treatment guidelines

  1. Reduce dose of drugs slowly

  2. Wean each drug by 10-15% of total daily dose.

  3. Do not wean drugs of different class on same day.

  4. Wean every other day if tolerated.

  5. Treat withdrawal symptoms by resuming previous dose or administering extra dose of drug.

  6. Clonidine may prevent or treat autonomic signs and symptoms of withdrawal
