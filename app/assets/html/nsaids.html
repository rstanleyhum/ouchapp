Common contraindications

  1. Anticoagulants

  2. Thrombolytic Rx

  3. Risk of critical bleeding

  4. Renal insufficiency

  5. Gastropathy

  6. Hepatopathy

  7. Combined toxicity with other drugs (i.e., multiple nephrotoxic drugs)

  8. Drug-drug interactions

  9. Low cardiac output

  10. And as per other references

Note
 Check with surgery team before prescribing ketoralac. Not to exceed 5 days. If >= 48 hrs, give with PPI
